Student of Computer Science

I am an undergraduate student and a perpetual optimist, my confidence in that statement only having grown given that I still am an optimist in late 2020. I am someone who loves to think- I enjoy cooking up algorithms to do things regardless of whether or not they’re better than ones that already exist- after all, you will never find gold if you’re not willing to dig.

I enjoy the mathematical aspects of Computer Science, and am interested in leveraging the power of big data and machine learning in order to solve real world problems.
